Frequently Asked Questions

Can i choose the dentist on my own for treatment ?

Yes, you can choose your dentist. We believe in giving our patients the freedom to select a dentist they feel comfortable with. You can request a specific dentist when booking your appointment, and we'll do our best to accommodate your preference.

Will i get the same doctor each time i visit the same clinic ?

We strive to provide continuity of care by scheduling you with the same dentist for all your visits. However, this may depend on the availability of the dentist and the urgency of your appointment. We'll always try our best to match you with your preferred dentist.

How you are able to provide less prices than other dental brands ?

We're able to offer competitive pricing thanks to our efficient operations, centralized procurement systems, and volume of patients we serve. We believe in transparent pricing and pass on these savings to our patients without compromising on the quality of care or materials used.

How you are able to provide less prices than other dental brands ?

We maintain affordable prices by optimizing our operational costs and leveraging our network of clinics for better negotiation power with suppliers. We focus on providing value-based care without hidden costs, ensuring our patients receive high-quality treatments at reasonable rates.

What are your dental clinic timings?

Our clinics typically operate from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM on weekdays and 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM on weekends. However, timings may vary slightly between locations. We recommend checking the specific hours for your nearest clinic on our website or by calling our customer care.

How can I book an appointment?

Booking an appointment is easy! You can schedule your visit through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer care number. You can also walk into any of our clinics, and our team will assist you in booking an appointment at your convenient time.

How frequently should I visit a dentist?

We recommend visiting a dentist every six months for regular check-ups and professional cleaning. However, the frequency may vary based on your oral health condition. Some patients with specific dental issues might need more frequent visits, while those with excellent oral health might require less frequent check-ups.
